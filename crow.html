The Thirsty Crow

Once upon a time, on a hot summer day, a thirsty crow was searching for water. After a long search, he finally found a pitcher in a garden.

The crow was excited but soon realized that the water level was too low for him to reach with his beak. He thought hard about how to solve this problem.

Suddenly, he had an idea! He looked around and saw some pebbles nearby. The clever crow began picking up the pebbles, one by one, and dropped them into the pitcher.

With each pebble he dropped, the water level rose a little higher. Finally, after several pebbles, the water level was high enough for the crow to drink.

The crow drank the water and felt refreshed. He was proud of his cleverness and learned that thinking creatively can solve difficult problems.

From that day on, the thirsty crow became known as a wise bird, reminding everyone that ingenuity can help overcome challenges.
